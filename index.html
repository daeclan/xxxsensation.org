HELPING HAND


XXXTENTACION (XT) came into the game with #BADVIBESFOREVER as his mantra. His whole aesthetic was negativity, violence, and misogny. As a counterpoint, XXXSENSATION's (XS) position was #GOODVIBESFOREVER and his songs, tweets, and merch reflected this positivity and contrast.


As XT's star grew, he began to shift his energy away from negativity and, in doing so, started copying the very actions that XS had done months prior. Most notably, in May 2017 XS began a "hashtag challenge"—#OneMillionForOurWomen—to raise $1,000,000 for women's rights and victims of domestic abuse. The challenge exchanged $5 donation receipts to women's rights or domestic violence nonp-profits with a 24 hour repost of XS's Soundcloud, and by the end of 2017, had raised over $1000.


Around the same time that the #OneMillionForOurWomen began, XS made a parody of XT's Members Only Vol. III, entitled Memebers Only Vol III. XT's hand and hand tattoo (which read KILL) was featured prominently, where XS's tattoo read "HELP". XS also released merch around this time featuring the hand tattoo literally named "HELPING HAND" (hyperlink).


Two weeks later, after #OneMillionForOurWomen and the HELPING HAND merch was released, XXXTENTACION reached out to XXXSENSATION via a Twitter DM applauding the work, making it clear he was aware of the work and encouraging it to continue:


Eight months later, in January 2018, XXXTENTACION began #TheHelpingHandChallenge — a "hashtag challenge" of his own that asked his followers to do good for people in need.
